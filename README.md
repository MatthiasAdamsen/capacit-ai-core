# Capacit AI Core 🚀

### *More than AI - Delivering Impactful Solutions*

**Capacit AI Core** is a comprehensive framework designed to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical business implementation. This repository serves as the central hub for AI solutions developed at **Capacit**, focusing on high-quality, scalable, and impactful applications.

---

## 🌟 Core Pillars

### 🤖 LLM & Generative AI
Harnessing the power of the latest Large Language Models to create intelligent agents, automated workflows, and advanced natural language interfaces.
- **Agents:** Autonomous task-oriented AI agents.
- **RAG:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation for specialized knowledge bases.

### 📊 Statistical Analysis & Classical ML
Diving deep into data with rigorous statistical methods. We believe in the power of the classics—from Random Forests to advanced Bayesian modeling.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Tabular data forecasting and classification.
- **Insights:** Extracting meaningful patterns from complex datasets.

### ⚙️ Engineering Excellence
Building robust pipelines that ensure AI solutions are performant, reliable, and easy to deploy.
- **Data Pipelines:** Streamlined ETL for AI readiness.
- **Deployment:** Containerized solutions for cloud and on-premise environments.
